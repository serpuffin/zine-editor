### Shehroz Urdu Font ‚Äî Neographic Experiment
The Urdu font is a deeper experiment in **neography**.

Instead of following existing Urdu scripts, this work explores what happens when you try to *invent a new way of writing Urdu*. Letterforms, joins, and structure are questioned ‚Äî and sometimes broken entirely.

This is not meant to replace traditional Urdu writing. It‚Äôs an exploration of what Urdu *could* look like if the rules were different.

Readability is secondary. Exploration comes first.
